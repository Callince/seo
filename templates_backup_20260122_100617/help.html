Help Center

Comprehensive guide to using Fourth Dimension SEO Dada. Learn how to use our powerful analysis tools, manage your account, and optimize your website for search engines.

Welcome to Fourth Dimension SEO Dada

Our platform helps you analyze websites comprehensively with tools for link analysis, SEO optimization, content analysis, and more. Get started by signing up, choosing a subscription plan, and exploring our powerful analysis tools.

Analysis Tools Overview

Fourth Dimension provides six powerful analysis tools to help you understand and optimize websites:

URL Search (1 Token)

Analyze all links on a webpage, categorize internal vs external links, and respect robots.txt rules. Navigate to URL Search, enter the website URL, choose whether to respect robots.txt, and click Analyze.

Keyword Analysis (3 Tokens)

Extract text content and analyze keyword density, frequency, and distribution across the page. Enter up to 10 keywords (comma-separated) and get comprehensive keyword insights including density percentages and frequency counts.

Image Analysis (2 Tokens)

Find all images on a page with details like file size, dimensions, alt text, and optimization status. Perfect for identifying image optimization opportunities and ensuring all images have proper alt text for accessibility.

Heading Analysis (1 Token)

Examine the heading structure (H1-H6) for SEO optimization and content hierarchy. Ensure your pages follow proper heading hierarchy and include target keywords in headings.

Meta Tag Analysis (2 Tokens)

Extract and analyze meta tags, Open Graph data, and JSON-LD schema markup. Optimize your title tags (50-60 characters), meta descriptions (150-160 characters), and social media tags.

Site Structure (2 Tokens)

Comprehensive website crawling with visual representation of site architecture. Start from any URL and the crawler will follow all internal links, respect robots.txt rules, check HTTP status codes, and build a complete site map.

Account Management

Profile Management

Update your personal information, change passwords, and manage account settings. Navigate to Profile from the main menu to update your name, change password (requires current password), and view account activity.

Password Security

Your password must meet these requirements:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • Contains uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Includes at least one number
  • Has at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

Email Verification

If you haven't verified your email or need to resend verification, go to the login page, click "Resend Verification", enter your email address, and check your inbox for the verification link.

Subscriptions & Token System

Understanding Tokens

Our platform uses a token-based system. Each analysis tool consumes a certain number of tokens. You get daily tokens with your subscription, and can purchase additional tokens when needed. Daily tokens reset every 24 hours, while additional tokens expire when your subscription ends.

Subscription Plans

Visit the Subscriptions page to view available plans and features. Each plan includes:

  • Daily token allocation
  • Access to all analysis tools
  • Search history and exports
  • Email support

Purchasing Additional Tokens

To purchase additional tokens:

  1. Go to your Subscriptions page
  2. Click "Purchase Additional Tokens"
  3. Select the number of tokens you need
  4. Complete payment via Razorpay
  5. Tokens are immediately added to your account

Dashboard

Your dashboard provides an overview of your account activity and usage statistics including daily token usage and remaining tokens, total analyses performed, most used analysis tools, and weekly usage trends.

Subscription Overview

The dashboard shows your current subscription status including active plan details, token usage progress, subscription expiry date, and auto-renewal status.

Search History

Track all your previous analyses and easily re-run them or reference past results. Click Search History in the main menu to view a chronological list of all analyses. Filter by date range or analysis tool, and click on any entry to view details.

History Information

Each entry includes:

  • URL Analyzed: The website or page examined
  • Tool Used: Which analysis tool was used
  • Date & Time: When the analysis was performed
  • Search Count: Number of times this URL was analyzed

Exports & Downloads

Export your analysis results in various formats for further processing or reporting. Download structured CSV data for link analysis results, image information, heading structure, meta tag data, and site structure.

How to Export Data

  1. Complete any analysis using our tools
  2. Look for the Download or Export button
  3. Choose your preferred format (usually CSV)
  4. File will download automatically

Troubleshooting

Analysis Not Working

Possible Causes:

  • Website blocks automated crawling
  • Invalid or non-existent URL
  • Insufficient tokens remaining
  • Network connectivity issues

Solutions:

  • Verify the URL is correct and accessible
  • Check your token balance
  • Try a different URL to test functionality
  • Contact support if issues persist

Access Denied Errors

Common Causes:

  • Subscription expired
  • Daily token limit reached
  • Account not verified

Solutions:

  • Check subscription status
  • Purchase additional tokens
  • Verify your email address
  • Renew expired subscription

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I run out of daily tokens?

When you exhaust your daily token allocation, you can either purchase additional tokens immediately or wait until the next day when your daily tokens reset. Additional tokens can be purchased in bundles of 10, 25, 50, or 100.

Can I analyze password-protected websites?

Our tools can only analyze publicly accessible web pages. Password-protected, login-required, or private areas of websites cannot be analyzed as our crawlers don't have authentication credentials.

How accurate is the SEO analysis?

Our analysis follows current SEO best practices and guidelines. However, SEO is complex and involves many factors. Use our results as guidance alongside other SEO tools and professional expertise for comprehensive optimization.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from the Subscriptions page. You'll continue to have access until your current billing period ends. Auto-renewal can be disabled without canceling immediately.
