Types of Website Structures

Different sites follow different layouts. Sitemap analysis helps you see which structure you currently have and whether it matches your goals.

Hierarchical Structure

Pages are arranged in parent and child groups with clear categories.

Best for large websites.

Flat Structure

Most pages are only a few clicks from the homepage.

Best for small sites.

Silo Structure

Pages are grouped by topic with strong internal linking.

Best for blogs and content-heavy sites.
